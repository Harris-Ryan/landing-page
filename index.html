Types of Climbing

Bouldering - Short, technichal routes with no rope

Top Roping - Longer routes and higher up with rope anchored to the top

Lead - Like top roping but you place your own protection via "quick-draws"

Ice Climbing - Like lead but you use ice picks on your hands and feet
